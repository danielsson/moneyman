Train the predictor

In order to be able to predict categories for your transactions, you'll need to train the predictor. This is done by manually assigning categories to your purchases. The machine learning system will automagically learn how to categorize your transactions.

Please upload a sample of your transactions

The accepted format is a CSV-file in the following format: 

2013-04-31, Transaction message, amount
We support all separator characters. About 100 rows will be selected for classification.
